Since \(T\) preserves antipodal points, \(T(0)\cdot \overline{T(\infty)} = -1\text{;}\) therefore, we have

\begin{equation*} \frac{pp_a - \lambda pp_a}{p_a - \lambda p} \cdot \frac{\overline{p}-\overline{\lambda}\overline{p_a}}{1-\overline{\lambda}} = -1\text{.} \end{equation*}
