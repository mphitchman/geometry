First, translate the point of rotation (\(2i)\) to the origin. Second, rotate by \(\pi/4\text{.}\) Third, translate the origin back to \(2i\text{.}\)

\begin{align*} z \amp\mapsto z - 2i \\ \amp\mapsto e^{i\frac{\pi}{4}}(z - 2i) \\ \amp\mapsto e^{i\frac{\pi}{4}}(z - 2i) + 2i \end{align*}

which can be written as

\begin{equation*} T(z) = \frac{e^{i\frac{\pi}{4}}z+(2i-e^{i\frac{\pi}{4}}2i)}{0z + 1}\text{.} \end{equation*}
