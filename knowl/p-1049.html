Recall, the unit 2-sphere \(\mathbb{S}^2\) consists of all points \((a,b,c)\) in \(\mathbb{R}^3\) for which \(a^2 + b^2 + c^2 = 1\text{,}\) and \(\mathbb{S}^2\) may be mapped onto the extended plane by the stereographic projection map \(\phi: \mathbb{S}^2 \to \mathbb{C}^+\) defined by

\begin{equation*} \phi(a,b,c) = \begin{cases}\frac{a}{1-c}+\frac{b}{1-c}i \amp \text{ if \(c \neq 1\); } \\ \infty \amp \text{ if \(c=1\) } \end{cases}\text{.} \end{equation*}
