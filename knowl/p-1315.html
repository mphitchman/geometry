Suppose a team of two-dimensional explorers living in \((X_k,G_k)\) finds themselves at point \(p\text{.}\) They travel 8 units along a line to a point \(z\text{,}\) turn right (90\(^\circ\)) and travel another 8 units along a line to the point \(q\text{.}\) At this point they measure \(\angle pzq=.789\) radians. Which type of geometry applies to their universe? Can they determine the value of \(k\) from this measurement? If so, what is it?
