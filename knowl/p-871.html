The hyperbolic line segment from 0 to \(c\) can be parameterized by \(\boldsymbol{r}_0(t) = x(t) + 0i\) for \(a \leq t \leq b\text{,}\) which has length

\begin{equation*} {\cal L}(\boldsymbol{r}_0) = \int_a^b\frac{2}{1-[x(t)]^2}\sqrt{(x^\prime(t))^2}~dt\text{.} \end{equation*}
