The result follows from properties of conjugates.

\begin{align*} \overline{(z,z_1;z_2,z_3)}\amp= \overline{\left[\frac{z-z_2}{z-z_3}\cdot\frac{z_1-z_3}{z_1-z_2}\right]}\\ \amp= \frac{\overline{z}-\overline{z_2}}{\overline{z}-\overline{z_3}}\cdot\frac{\overline{z_1}-\overline{z_3}}{\overline{z_1}-\overline{z_2}}\\ \amp= (\overline{z},\overline{z_1};\overline{z_2},\overline{z_3})\text{.} \end{align*}
