Note that by the quotient rule,

\begin{align*} T^\prime(z) \amp = e^{i\theta}\frac{(1-\overline{z_0}z)+(z-z_0)\overline{z_0}}{(1-\overline{z_0}z)^2}\\ \amp = e^{i\theta}\frac{1-|z_0|^2}{(1-\overline{z_0}z)^2}\text{.} \end{align*}

By the chain rule,

\begin{equation*} \boldsymbol{s}^\prime(t) = T^\prime(\boldsymbol{r}(t))\cdot \boldsymbol{r}^\prime(t)\text{,} \end{equation*}

or

\begin{equation*} \boldsymbol{s}^\prime(t) = e^{i\theta}\frac{1-|z_0|^2}{(1-\overline{z_0}\boldsymbol{r}(t))^2}\cdot \boldsymbol{r}^\prime(t)\text{.} \end{equation*}
