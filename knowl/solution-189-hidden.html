As suggested by Figure 7.5.27, reflect rectangle 1 about a vertical axis, reflect rectangle 3 about a horizontal axis, and rotate rectangle 2 by 180\(^\circ\text{,}\) and then reassemble the four rectangles as indicated. The result is a new rectangle whose vertical sides, labeled $a_1$, are identified directly, as with the torus, and whose horizontal sides are identified with a twist. The result is the Klein bottle of Example 7.5.15.
