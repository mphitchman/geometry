Suppose \(T(z) = z + b\text{.}\) Then \(v(T(z_1),T(z_2)) = (z_1+b)-(z_2+b) = z_1 - z_2 = v(z_1,z_2)\text{,}\) so \(v\) is invariant under translations. However, if \(R_\theta(z) = e^{i\theta}z\) then

\begin{align*} v(R_\theta(z_1),R_\theta(z_2)) \amp = e^{i\theta}z_1 - e^{i\theta}z_2\\ \amp = e^{i\theta}(z_1-z_2) \\ \amp \neq z_1 - z_2 \amp \text{when } e^{i\theta} \neq 1 \text{.} \end{align*}
