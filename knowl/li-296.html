Item 7.3.4.b.
Using the expressions for \(\sinh(\sqrt{|k|}a)\) and \(\sinh(\sqrt{|k|}b)\) from part (a), and the correct expressions for the symmetric points of \(p = r\) and \(q = me^{i\gamma}\) (\(p^* = -\frac{1}{kr}\) and \(q^* = -\frac{1}{km}e^{i\gamma}\)) the general hyperbolic law of sines may be proved by following the proof of Exercise 5.4.12.
