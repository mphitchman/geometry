Now,

\begin{equation*} |T(q)|= \bigg|\frac{q - p}{1+\overline{p}q}\bigg|\text{,} \end{equation*}

and the reader can check, using the fact that \(q_a = -1/\overline{q}\text{,}\) that

\begin{equation*} |T(q_a)| = \bigg|\frac{1+\overline{p}q}{q - p}\bigg|. \end{equation*}

It follows that

\begin{equation*} d_S(p,q)=~\text{min}\bigg\{2\arctan\bigg(\bigg|\frac{q - p}{1+\overline{p}q}\bigg|\bigg), 2\arctan\bigg(\bigg|\frac{1+\overline{p}q}{q-p}\bigg|\bigg)\bigg\}. \end{equation*}
