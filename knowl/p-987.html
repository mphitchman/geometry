If we expand the following product

\begin{equation*} \phi(P) \cdot \overline{\phi(Q)} = \bigg[\frac{a}{1-c} +\frac{b}{1-c} i\bigg]\cdot \bigg[\frac{-a}{1+c} + \frac{b}{1+c} i\bigg], \end{equation*}

we obtain

\begin{equation*} \phi(P) \cdot \overline{\phi(Q)} = -\frac{a^2 + b^2}{1-c^2} \end{equation*}

which reduces to \(-1\) since \(a^2+b^2+c^2=1\text{.}\)
