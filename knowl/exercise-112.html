Exercise 1.

Properties of \(\sinh(x) = (e^x-e^{-x})/2\) and \(\cosh(x)=(e^x+e^{-x})/2\).

a. Verify that \(\sinh(0) = 0\) and \(\cosh(0) = 1\text{.}\)

b. Verify that \(\frac{d}{dx}[\sinh(x)]=\cosh(x)\) and \(\frac{d}{dx}[\cosh(x)]=\sinh(x)\text{.}\)

c. Verify that \(\cosh^2(x)-\sinh^2(x)=1\text{.}\)

d. Verify that the power series expansions for \(\cosh(x)\) and \(\sinh(x)\) are

\begin{align*} \cosh(x)\amp =1+\frac{x^2}{2}+\frac{x^4}{4!}+\cdots\\ \sinh(x)\amp =x+\frac{x^3}{3!}+\frac{x^5}{5!}+\cdots. \end{align*}
