Suppose \(z_0\) is the origin. Then reflection about the horizontal line \(L_1\) is \(r_1(z) = \overline{z}\) by Exercise 3.1.8, and reflection about \(L_2\) is \(r_2(z) = e^{i\theta}\overline{z}\) by Exercise 3.1.9. Thus,

\begin{equation*} r_2(r_1(z))=e^{i\theta}z \end{equation*}

which is rotation about the origin by angle \(\theta\text{.}\)
