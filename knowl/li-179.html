Item 5.3.6.a.
If \(\boldsymbol{r}(t) = x(t) + i y(t)\) is a smooth curve in \(\mathbb{D}\) its image under reflection about the \(x\)-axis is \(\overline{\boldsymbol{r}(t)} = x(t) - i y(t)\text{,}\) and \(|\boldsymbol{r}(t)| = |\overline{\boldsymbol{r}(t)}|\text{.}\) Moreover, \(\overline{\boldsymbol{r}^\prime(t)} = x^\prime(t) - i y^\prime(t)\) so \(|\boldsymbol{r}^\prime(t)| = |\overline{\boldsymbol{r}^\prime(t)}|\) and it follows that the the length of \(\boldsymbol{r}\) is equal to the length of \(\overline{\boldsymbol{r}}\text{.}\)
