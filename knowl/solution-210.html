Solution 8.2.5.1.
  1. According to Gauss-Bonnet, the area of the Dirichlet domain is
    \begin{equation*} A_d = \frac{2\pi (2-g)}{k} = 2\pi(g-2)\text{.} \end{equation*}
  2. The area of a circle in \((\mathbb{D},{\cal H})\) with radius \(r_\text{obs}\) is
    \begin{equation*} A_\circ = 4\pi \sinh^2(r_\text{obs}/2)\text{.} \end{equation*}
  3. In general,
    \begin{equation*} \frac{A_\circ}{A_d}=2\frac{\sinh^2(r_\text{obs}/2)}{g-2}\text{.} \end{equation*}
