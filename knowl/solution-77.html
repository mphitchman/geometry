Solution 3.5.11.1.

By Theorem 3.2.16 there are two points \(p\) and \(q\) symmetric with respect to both \(C_1\) and \(C_2\text{.}\) The composition thus fixes these two points. Moroever, any cline that goes through both \(p\) and \(q\) must be orthogonal to \(C_1\) (and \(C_2\)) by Theorem 3.2.8.
