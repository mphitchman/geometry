Item 2.1.3.a.
Suppose \(z = x+yi\) and \(w = s+ti\text{.}\) Then
\begin{align*} |zw| \amp = \sqrt{(xs-yt)^2 + (xt+ys)^2}\\ \amp = \sqrt{x^2s^2 - 2xyst + y^2t^2 + x^2t^2 + 2xyst + y^2s^2}\\ \amp = \sqrt{x^2(s^2+t^2)+y^2(s^2 + t^2)} \\ \amp = \sqrt{(x^2 + y^2)(s^2+t^2)} \\ \amp = |z|\cdot |w|\text{.} \end{align*}
