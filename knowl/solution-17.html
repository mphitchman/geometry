Solution 2.3.4.1.

To prove \(\Delta 01z\) and \(\Delta0wu\) are similar, notice that

\begin{align*} \angle u0w \amp = \text{arg}(u)-\text{arg}(w)\\ \amp = (\theta+\alpha)-\alpha\\ \amp = \theta\text{,} \end{align*}

so that \(\angle u0w = \angle z01\text{.}\) Next, notice that

\begin{equation*} \frac{|1-0|}{|w-0|} = \frac{1}{|w|} \end{equation*}

and that

\begin{equation*} \frac{|z-0|}{|u-0|} = \frac{|z|}{|u|} = \frac{|z|}{|zw|} = \frac{1}{|w|}\text{.} \end{equation*}

That is, the two triangles have two equal side ratios with equal included angles, so the triangles are similar.
