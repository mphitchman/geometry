To get back to the distance function \(d(x)\) we integrate:

\begin{align*} \int \frac{2}{1-x^2} ~dx \amp = \int \bigg( \frac{1}{1-x} + \frac{1}{1+x}\bigg)~dx \tag{partial~fractions}\\ \amp = -\ln(1-x) + \ln(1+x)\\ d(x) \amp = \ln\bigg(\frac{1+x}{1-x}\bigg), \end{align*}
