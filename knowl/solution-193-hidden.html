By Gauss-Bonnet, the curvature of the 3-holed torus is

\begin{equation*} k = \frac{2\pi(-4)}{5.2}~\text{km}^{-2}\text{.} \end{equation*}

The area of the surface \(C_3\) with this curvature, and whose Euler characteristic is -1, is thus

\begin{equation*} A = \frac{2\pi(-1)}{\frac{2\pi(-4)}{5.2}}=\frac{5.2}{4}=1.3 ~\text{km}^2\text{.} \end{equation*}
