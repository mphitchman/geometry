Let

\begin{equation*} T_1(z) = \frac{z-\frac{1}{2}}{1+\frac{1}{2}z}~~~\text{ and }~~~ T_2(z) = \frac{z-(\frac{1}{2}+\frac{1}{2}i)}{1+(\frac{1}{2}-\frac{1}{2}i)z}\text{.} \end{equation*}

Then the map \(T_2^{-1}\circ T_1(z)\) sends \(\frac{1}{2}\) to \(\frac{1}{2}+\frac{1}{2}i\text{.}\) The map \(T^{-1}_2\) is given by

\begin{equation*} T^{-1}_2(z)=\frac{-z-(\frac{1}{2}+\frac{1}{2}i)}{(\frac{1}{2}-\frac{1}{2}i)z - 1}\text{,} \end{equation*}

and one checks that

\begin{align*} T_2^{-1}\circ T_1(z) \amp= \frac{\frac{z - \frac{1}{2}}{1 + \frac{1}{2}z}+\frac{1}{2}+\frac{1}{2}i}{1-(\frac{1}{2}-\frac{1}{2}i)\frac{z-\frac{1}{2}}{1+\frac{1}{2}z}}\\ \amp= \frac{(5+i)z + 2i}{2iz + 5 - i}\text{.} \end{align*}
