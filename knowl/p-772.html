Now, the quantity

\begin{equation*} \frac{\overline{z_0}z_1 - 1}{z_1-z_0} \end{equation*}

is a complex constant, and it has modulus equal to 1. To see this, observe that since \(1 = |z_1|^2 = z_1\overline{z_1}\text{,}\)

\begin{align*} \frac{\overline{z_0}z_1 - 1}{z_1 - z_0}\amp =\frac{\overline{z_0}z_1 - z_1\overline{z_1}}{z_1 - z_0}\\ \amp = \frac{-z_1(\overline{z_1 - z_0})}{z_1-z_0}\text{.} \end{align*}
