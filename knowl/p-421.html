Check that \(\Delta zms\) is similar to \(\Delta z^*ms\) and \(\Delta zmt\) is similar to \(\Delta z^*mt\text{.}\) It follows that \(\angle szm = \angle sz^*m\) and \(\angle mzt = \angle mz^*t\text{.}\) Thus, \(\angle szt = \angle tz^*s\text{.}\)
