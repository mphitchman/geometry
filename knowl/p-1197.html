Let \(m_r\) be the midpoint of the segment connecting \(r\) and \(-1/r\text{,}\) and let \(m_q\) be the midpoint of the segment connecting \(q\) and \(q_a\text{.}\) So,

\begin{equation*} |m_r - r| = \frac{1}{2}\bigg(r+\frac{1}{r}\bigg) = \frac{1+r^2}{2r}\text{,} \end{equation*}

and

\begin{equation*} |m_q - q| = \bigg|\frac{1}{2}\bigg(k+\frac{1}{k}\bigg)e^{i\gamma}\bigg| = \frac{1+k^2}{2k}\text{.} \end{equation*}
