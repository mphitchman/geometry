Solution 5.5.4.1.

The region may be described in Cartesian coordinates by \(0 \leq x \leq 1\) and \(1 \leq y \leq x + 1\) so its area is

\begin{align*} A \amp= \int_0^1 \int_1^{x+1}\frac{1}{y^2}~dy~dx \\ \amp= \int_0^1 1 - \frac{1}{x+1}~dx\\ \amp= x - \ln(x+1) \bigg|_0^1 \\ \amp= 1 - \ln(2)\text{.} \end{align*}
