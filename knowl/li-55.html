Special cases of general linear transformations:

  • Translation by \(b\text{:}\) \(T_b(z) = z + b\text{.}\)
  • Rotation by \(\theta\) about \(0\text{:}\) \(R_\theta(z)=e^{i\theta}z\text{.}\)
  • Rotation by \(\theta\) about \(z_0\text{:}\) \(R(z)=e^{i\theta}(z-z_0)+z_0\text{.}\)
  • Dilation by factor \(k \gt 0\text{:}\) \(T(z)=kz\text{.}\)
