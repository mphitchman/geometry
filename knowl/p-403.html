\(T_1(z) = \frac{r_1^2}{\overline{z}}\) and \(T_2(z) = \frac{r_2^2}{\overline{z}}\text{,}\) so

\begin{equation*} T_2\circ T_1(z) = \frac{r_2^2}{\overline{r_1^2/\overline{z}}} = \bigg(\frac{r_2}{r_1}\bigg)^2 z\text{.} \end{equation*}

The composition is a stretch by factor \((r_2/r_1)^2\text{.}\)
