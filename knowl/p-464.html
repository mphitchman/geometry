There is an algebraic description of the very useful Möbius transformation mapping \(z_1 \mapsto 1\text{,}\) \(z_2 \mapsto 0\) and \(z_3 \mapsto \infty\) that arose in the proof of Theorem 3.4.8:

\begin{equation*} T(z) = \frac{(z-z_2)}{(z-z_3)}\cdot \frac{(z_1 - z_3)}{(z_1-z_2)}. \end{equation*}
