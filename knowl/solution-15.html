Solution 2.3.2.1.

Converting fractions to Cartesian form:

\begin{equation*} \frac{1}{2i} = -\frac{1}{2}i\text{,} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \frac{1}{1+i}=\frac{1}{2}-\frac{1}{2}i\text{,} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \frac{4+i}{1-2i}=\frac{2}{5}+\frac{9}{5}i\text{,} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \frac{2}{3+i} = \frac{3}{5}-\frac{1}{5}i\text{.} \end{equation*}
