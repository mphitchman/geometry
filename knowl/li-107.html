Item 3.5.9.c.
If \(T\) is loxodromic, \(T\) has normal form
\begin{equation*} \frac{T(z)-p}{T(z)-q} = re^{i\theta}\frac{z-p}{z-q} \end{equation*}
and letting \(z = z_\infty\) we have
\begin{equation*} z_\infty-q = re^{i\theta}(z_\infty-p)\text{.} \end{equation*}
The vectors \(z_\infty - q\) and \(z_\infty - p\) are perpendicular if \(\theta = \pi/2\) or \(3\pi/2\text{.}\) In this case, by LemmaÂ 3.5.7, it follows that the four points \(p,q,z_\infty,w_\infty\) form a rectangle.
