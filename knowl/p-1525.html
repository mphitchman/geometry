For \(g \geq 2\text{,}\) \(H_g\) can be constructed from \(2g-2\) pairs of pants. Thinking of \(H_g\) as a string of \(g\) donuts, the donut on each end is formed from 1 pair of pants, and each of the \(g-2\) “middle” donuts is formed from 2 pairs of pants, as suggested by the cuts in the following diagram TODO. This means the \(g\)-holed torus is constructed from \(2(g-2) + 2 = 2g-2\) pairs of pants.
