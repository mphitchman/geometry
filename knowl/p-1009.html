The pre-image of the circle \(|w - (3 + 2i)| = 4\) in \(\mathbb{C}\) is the circle on \(\mathbb{S}^2\) defined by the plane

\begin{equation*} -2 \cdot 3 x - 2\cdot 2 y + (1 - 13+4) z + (1 + 13 - 4) = 0 \end{equation*}

or

\begin{equation*} -3x - 2y - 4z + 5 = 0. \end{equation*}
