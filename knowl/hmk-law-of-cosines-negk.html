Triangle trigonometry in \((\mathbb{D}_k,{\cal H}_k)\text{.}\)

Suppose we have a triangle in \((\mathbb{D}_k,{\cal H}_k)\) with side lengths \(a,b,c\) and angles \(\alpha, \beta,\) and \(\gamma\) as pictured in FigureÂ 5.4.11. Suppose further that \(s = 1/\sqrt{|k|}\text{.}\)

  1. Prove the hyperbolic law of cosines in \((\mathbb{D}_k,{\cal H}_k)\text{:}\)
    \begin{equation*} \cosh(c/s)=\cosh(a/s)\cosh(b/s)-\sinh(a/s)\sinh(b/s)\cos(\gamma)\text{.} \end{equation*}
  2. Prove the hyperbolic law of sines in \((\mathbb{D}_k,{\cal H}_k)\text{:}\)
    \begin{equation*} \frac{\sinh(a/s)}{\sin(\alpha)}=\frac{\sinh(b/s)}{\sin(\beta)}=\frac{\sinh(c/s)}{\sin(\gamma)}\text{.} \end{equation*}
