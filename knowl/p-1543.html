Rather than hunt for two copies of the same source, we compute the distance between each pair of sources in the catalog (we must make an assumption about the geometry of space to compute these distances), giving us \(N(N-1)/2\) distances. If the catalog contains no repeat images, then the distances ought to follow a Poisson probability distribution.
