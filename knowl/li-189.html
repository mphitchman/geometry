Item 5.4.1.b.

The derivatives are computed as follows:

\begin{align*} \frac{d}{dx}[\sinh(x)] \amp = \frac{d}{dx}\bigg[\frac{1}{2}e^x - \frac{1}{2}e^{-x}\bigg]\\ \amp= \frac{1}{2}e^x + \frac{1}{2}e^{-x}\\ \amp= \cosh(x)\text{.} \end{align*}
\begin{align*} \frac{d}{dx}[\cosh(x)] \amp= \frac{d}{dx}\bigg[\frac{1}{2}e^x + \frac{1}{2}e^{-x}\bigg]\\ \amp= \frac{1}{2}e^x - \frac{1}{2}e^{-x}\\ \amp= \sinh(x)\text{.} \end{align*}
