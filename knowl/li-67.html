Item 3
Transitivity: Suppose \(z \sim w\) and \(w \sim v\text{.}\) We must show \(z \sim v\text{.}\) Since \(z \sim w\text{,}\) Re\((z) - ~\text{Re}(w) = k\) for some integer \(k\text{,}\) and since \(w \sim v\text{,}\) Re\((w) - ~\text{Re}(v) = l\) for some integer \(l\text{.}\) Notice that
\begin{align*} k+l \amp =[\text{Re}(z)-~\text{Re}(w)]+[\text{Re}(w) - ~\text{Re}(v)]\\ \amp = \text{Re}(z) - ~\text{Re}(v). \end{align*}
So, Re\((z) - ~\text{Re}(v)\) is an integer. Furthermore, we have Im\((z) =\) Im\((w) =\) Im\((v)\text{.}\) Thus, \(z \sim v\text{.}\)
