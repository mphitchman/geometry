Solution 7.5.1.1.
  1. Traverse the corners to see all 6 come together at a point, so that the cell division has 1 vertex. Of course, the cell division has 3 edges and 1 face, so \(\chi = -1\) and the space is \(C_3\text{.}\)
  2. The space is orientable with \(\chi = 1 - 4 + 1 = -2\text{,}\) so the surface is \(H_2\text{.}\)
  3. The space is non-orientable, with \(\chi = 2 - 4 + 1 = -1\text{,}\) so the surface is \(C_3\text{.}\)
  4. The space is non-orientable, and \(\chi = 1 - 5 + 1 = -3\text{,}\) so the surface is \(C_5\text{.}\)
