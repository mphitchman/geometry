Solution 3.5.4.1.
  1. The image of the cline through \(a,b,c\) is itself since the images of \(a,b,\) and \(c\) all live on this cline, and a cline is determined by three points.
  2. Note that \(T(T(T(z))) = z\) for \(z = a,b,c\) so the triple composition fixes three points and must be the identity.
  3. We may show, much as in the previous exercise, that \(T\) cannot fix just one point, and if \(T\) fixes two points then, in the normal form, \(\lambda^3 = 1\) so that the map is elliptic.
